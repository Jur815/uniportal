# UniPortal Executive Presentation Slides

PowerPoint-ready version for vice chancellors, registrars, deans, ICT directors, and governing boards.

Recommended deck style:

- **Theme:** Deep institutional blue, gold accents, white background.
- **Visual tone:** Modern university ERP/SIS, premium, minimal, trustworthy.
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif, strong headings, short executive bullets.
- **Layout:** Two-column slides where possible. Avoid dense paragraphs.
- **Branding:** UniPortal logo top-left or title slide center.
- **Accent use:** Gold only for key highlights, section markers, or important numbers.

Recommended colors:

- Deep blue: `#071f49`
- Corporate blue: `#0b2f6b`
- Gold accent: `#d4af37`
- Background: `#ffffff`
- Light section background: `#f5f7fb`
- Body text: `#172033`
- Muted text: `#6b7280`

## Slide Design Notes

Use consistent layout:

- Title at top-left.
- Subtitle under title.
- Left side: 3-6 short bullets.
- Right side: screenshot or visual.
- Bottom-right: small UniPortal logo or gold accent line.

Avoid:

- Long paragraphs.
- Too many screenshots on one slide.
- Technical implementation detail unless speaking to ICT.
- Overselling as a complete SIS.

Use:

- Blue section headers.
- Gold highlights for “Pilot”, “Audit Log”, “Enrollment Slip”, and “ROI”.
- White cards on light gray backgrounds.
- Large readable screenshots.

## Presenter Tone

Use this positioning:

“UniPortal is a practical institutional MVP. It proves the registration workflow and gives the university a foundation to move toward a full SIS through phased rollout.”

Avoid:

“This is a complete production SIS.”

Say instead:

“This is ready for institutional demonstration and pilot discovery. Full production rollout should follow a phased implementation plan.”
